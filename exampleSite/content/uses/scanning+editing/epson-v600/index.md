## Thoughts

The V600 is not the fastest scanner, but it is consistent and easy to keep calibrated for home workflows.

## Examples

- Weekly 35mm negative scanning before cataloging in Lightroom.
- Test scans to evaluate exposure choices before committing to full rolls.
